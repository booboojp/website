Who are you?
I am an aspiring programmer and/or electronics engineer! For more info, visit my About page.

Why is your website Galaga themed?
I really like Galaga! In my opinion, old video games did more with less; that is to say, they were incredibly fun even with the extremely limited resources. I also think that older games have a certain charm to them that hasn't truly been replicated since around the era of the Nintendo DS.

What are those little icons at the bottom of your site and where can I get them?
Those are called 88x31 GIFs (although some of mine are actually PNGs). I got them from friends' sites and the 88x31 archive. In the case of the W3C CSS certification, you can get that by verifying your site's CSS here (it's free).
